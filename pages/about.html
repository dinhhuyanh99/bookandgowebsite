About Us

Who
Established in the early months of 2019, Book'N'Go is a new and innovative event booking system. With a small team of only 5 University of Wollongong students, we currently are exclusively contracted to provide a fully fledged system that allows event managers to host events at the Univerisity of Wollongong and the general public to view and purchase tickets for these events. We strongly believe that our system is more robust and capable than previous event booking systems, if you don't believe us, try it out and we will show you!

What
At Book'N'Go we don't just provide a platform to host and book events. We provide a quality experience that is yet to be matched. User experience is highly regarded and we ensure that our interaction is fluent and responsive across all devices.
